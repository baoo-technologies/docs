## How Homie works (under the hood)

1. **Index** — Homie syncs your entire product catalog, CMS pages, and FAQs into a vector DB.
2. **Understand** — GPT‑4o analyses intent, sentiment, and purchase signals in real time.
3. **Answer & recommend** — combines factual answers with AI‑generated up‑ & cross‑sell suggestions.
4. **Learn** — every chat feeds analytics so you know which questions block sales.
