<Note>
  No app install needed. Works with every Online Store 2.0 theme (e.g. *Dawn*).
  Runtime footprint &lt; 5 KB gzipped & deferred.
</Note>

---

Open your assistant in the **Homie dashboard** → **Connect** and click <kbd>copy script</kbd>.

## 4 • Paste & save

1. In the sidebar, open **layout/theme.liquid**.
2. Scroll right to the bottom – just above `</body>`.
3. Paste the snippet you copied.

```liquid
<script
  src="https://cdn.yourhomie.ai/embed.min.js"
  chatbotId="YOUR_CHATBOT_ID"  <!-- already included in your copied snippet -->
  defer
></script>
```

<div className="my-4">
  <img
    src="/images/integrations/shopify/shopify-last-step.png"

Click **Speichern** – that’s it. Refresh your store and the Homie chat bubble appears in the bottom‑right corner. **Sales can start rolling in!**

---

### Troubleshooting

- **No bubble showing?** Clear cache or try an incognito window.
- **Multiple themes?** Add the snippet to each theme you publish.
- **Need a custom position or colour?** Adjust it in the Homie dashboard under **Settings → Chat interface**.
